About me

sadly, this is not my dog.

I've spent the last 8 years in the tech industry in various capacities, including project management, develepment, and operations. No matter where I am or what the project is, I'm happiest when I'm crafting creative, comprehensive solutions to interesting problems. If a penchant for ruthless efficiency and big-picture thinking are traits you can relate to, or at least appreciate, then we'll get along just fine.

In addition to providing freelance and consulting services, I presently serve as Director of Product Development for Legacy Sports, LLC, a growing multi-store Magento operation that provides fully-managed retail solutions to D3 athletic departments and other teams and organizations.

I breathe music and science fiction. I thoroughly enjoy hammering away at my piano and pretending my cats have coherent thoughts, à la The Bobcats. When I don't have my nose to the grindstone, you can find it sniffing around for a great craft beer or an amazing breakfast, for as the great Leslie Knope once queried, "Why would anyone ever eat anything other than breakfast food?"
